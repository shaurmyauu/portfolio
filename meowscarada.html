Meowscarada

This is a discord bot dedicated to the Pokemon theme, it has extensive functionality for catching Pokemon, feeding and caring.

Its main uniqueness lies in new features that no one has provided yet, beautiful UI design and more than 30+ items. Customization of Pokemon of your choice, giving it a gender, description or name. The system of evolution, trading and marketing, as well as quests and much more.

The bot will add an interesting plot in the future and also a battle system. More interesting information can be found on the official discord server.
